Our story

Capturing progress. Creating clarity.

BodenRyan specialises in timelapse, photography and site monitoring for construction projects of all sizes. The aim is simple: create a clear visual record that is useful during the build and valuable long after completion.

From single installations to long-term developments, the service combines robust camera systems, photography experience and a straightforward online portal. That makes it easier for project managers, stakeholders and marketing teams to access the right content at the right time.

Based in Nottingham and working across the UK, BodenRyan focuses on dependable delivery, clean visuals and systems that hold up in real site conditions.
