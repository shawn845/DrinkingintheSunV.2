MP Moran Warehouse & Headquarters

An 18-month solar-powered timelapse installation documenting the construction of a new warehouse and headquarters development in Watford.

Project overview

MP Moran builder merchants wanted to document the build of their new warehouse in Watford. The project needed a reliable long-term system that could run with minimal intervention while keeping the client connected to progress throughout the build.

The brief

Provide a solar-powered installation capable of running for the full project duration, with regular image uploads so the client could review progress and use new imagery in updates.

What we delivered
  • One rooftop-mounted solar-powered timelapse camera system
  • Image capture every ten minutes across an 18-month programme
  • Online viewer uploads for regular access and downloads
  • High-resolution imagery suitable for printing and updates
Outcome

The client was able to access fresh project imagery each week through the viewer, helping them document progress and communicate the build effectively while the system continued recording autonomously.

“Long-duration projects need dependable power, dependable framing and dependable access. That is where the system really pays off.”
