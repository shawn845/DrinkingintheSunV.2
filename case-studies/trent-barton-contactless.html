Project overview

East Midlands bus company Trent Barton became the first to roll out tap-on, tap-off contactless payments in the UK. The work took place over a weekend and needed concise visual coverage of activity inside the workshop environment.

The brief

Capture a short programme of works efficiently, showing both the wider workshop activity and the installation of ticketing machines and signage.

What we delivered
  • Two static timelapse cameras installed ahead of the works
  • Battery-powered systems used where mains power was unavailable
  • Custom roof-support mounting for the right overhead angle
  • Two ground-level timelapse cameras documenting installation details
Outcome

The final coverage documented a fast-moving operational change clearly and efficiently, creating useful visual content around a first-of-its-kind rollout.

“The setup needed to be fast, flexible and unobtrusive — exactly the kind of short programme where compact systems make a difference.”
