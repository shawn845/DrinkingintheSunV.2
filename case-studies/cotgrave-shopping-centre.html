Cotgrave Shopping Centre Redevelopment

Project overview

We were commissioned to record a £10.5m regeneration project in Cotgrave. The scheme delivered a new multi-service centre to house GPs, police, library and a Rushcliffe Borough Council contact point, while the former police station, library and health centre were removed to create a new landscaped public space.

The brief

Provide a clear visual record of the build and surrounding activity, covering the overall project while also capturing selected work activity on the ground.

What we delivered
  • One solar-powered timelapse camera capturing the full site
  • Supplementary ground-level footage during key stages of work
  • Online viewer access for project monitoring
  • Simple visual archive suitable for client updates and reporting
Outcome

The online viewer became a useful monitoring tool for the project manager, while the finished content provided a strong visual record of the redevelopment and its transformation into a welcoming public environment.

“The visual record was a useful way to track the project and clearly communicate progress.”
