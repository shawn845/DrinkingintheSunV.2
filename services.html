Services

1. Timelapse Production

Professional timelapse videos that bring your project to life. From installation to final edit, BodenRyan manages the technical workflow and finished delivery.

  • Scheduled recording tailored to the project programme
  • Monthly or milestone edits for progress reporting

2. Construction Photography

High-resolution photography capturing progress, detail and impact. Ideal for documentation, reporting, marketing and stakeholder communication.

  • Regular progress photography

3. Site Monitoring

Purpose-built monitoring systems providing secure and reliable visibility of your site from anywhere. Systems are built for the UK climate and flexible installation.

  • Solar-powered or mains-powered options
  • 4G, Wi-Fi or Ethernet connectivity
  • Remote control of settings and schedule
  • Weatherproof systems with backup storage

4. Online Portal & Reporting

Every project can be supported by a secure online viewer, giving your team access to imagery and downloads throughout the build.

  • Search and download high-resolution images 24/7

  • Three-location image backup approach
  • Simple access for project managers and stakeholders
